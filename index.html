About the band

Guitar Hero's became a global sensation by bringing digital legends to real-life stages. Fronted by Johnny Napalm, the band made history by selling out Wembley Stadium in minutes. They are now icons of stadium rock, famous for their high-energy performances and record-breaking crowd attendance.

The Band Pact

  • Silence: to be a hermetic band with no leaked information
  • Commitment: to be fully present on stage regardless of what's happening below
  • Pride: be proud of achieving your goals
  • Precision: To execute every note and beat with surgical accuracy, honoring the technical mastery that defines the band's legendary sound
  • Legacy: To build a timeless impact that transcends generations, ensuring the music remains a reference for future rock icons long after the tour ends.
